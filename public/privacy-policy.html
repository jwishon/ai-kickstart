Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 17, 2026

AI Kickstart ("the App") is an educational app that helps professionals learn to use AI tools effectively. Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what data the App collects, how it's used, and your rights.

Data We Collect

AI Kickstart stores your preferences and progress locally on your device using browser storage (localStorage). This includes:

  • Your selected role (e.g., Project Manager, Marketer)
  • Module completion progress
  • Saved prompt favorites
  • Assessment scores
  • Streak and last visit date

This data never leaves your device. We do not transmit, upload, or sync it to any server.

Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or process:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Contacts, photos, or files
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or telemetry

Accounts and Authentication

AI Kickstart does not require an account. There is no login, registration, or authentication of any kind.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. No data is shared with third parties.

Children's Privacy

AI Kickstart is rated for all ages. Because the App does not collect any personal information, it complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations.

Data Storage and Security

All data is stored locally on your device using standard browser storage. You can clear this data at any time by clearing your browser or app data through your device settings.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, the revised version will be posted at this URL with an updated effective date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at wishon@gmail.com.
